Cosmetic Dermatologist’s in London

Looking to refresh your appearance, smooth fine lines, or improve your skin’s tone and texture? Cosmetic dermatology offers advanced, non-surgical solutions to help you feel more confident in your skin—without the need for invasive procedures or extended downtime.

At the London Dermatology Centre, our consultant dermatologists specialise in cosmetic treatments that combine medical precision with aesthetic expertise. Whether you’re concerned about ageing, pigmentation, acne scarring, or skin texture, we offer a personalised approach to help you look and feel your best.

What Is a Cosmetic Dermatologist?

A cosmetic dermatologist is a medically qualified skin specialist who focuses on improving the appearance, tone, and texture of the skin. Unlike beauticians or therapists, cosmetic dermatologists are trained to assess your skin from both a clinical and aesthetic perspective—ensuring treatments are safe, medically appropriate, and results-driven.

At our clinic, your cosmetic consultation is led by a GMC-registered consultant dermatologist who understands the science behind your skin and the artistry of subtle, natural-looking improvements.

What Concerns Can We Help With?

Our cosmetic dermatology treatments are tailored to address a wide range of skin concerns, including:

  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Pigmentation and sun damage
  • Uneven skin tone or dull complexion
  • Acne and acne scarring
  • Redness or thread veins
  • Enlarged pores
  • Loss of firmness or elasticity
  • Volume loss in the face

Whether you’re looking for a one-off treatment or a longer-term skin rejuvenation plan, we’ll guide you through your options with expert care.

Cosmetic Treatments We Offer

Our clinic offers a full range of evidence-based cosmetic dermatology treatments, performed or overseen by our consultant dermatologists.

Anti-Wrinkle Injections

Target dynamic lines and wrinkles—such as frown lines, forehead lines, and crow’s feet—with quick, virtually painless injections.

  • Natural-looking results
  • No downtime
  • Effects typically last 3–4 months

Dermal Fillers

Restore volume to the cheeks, lips, and under-eye areas using hyaluronic acid-based fillers for a more youthful, balanced appearance.

  • Subtle, sculpted results
  • Minimal recovery time
  • Tailored to your facial structure

Chemical Peels

Exfoliate and renew the skin using professionally applied solutions that improve texture, reduce pigmentation, and stimulate collagen.

  • Range of depths available
  • Ideal for acne, sun damage, or dullness
  • Customised to your skin type

Microneedling

Stimulates natural collagen production using fine needles to reduce the appearance of scars, wrinkles, and enlarged pores.

  • Improves skin tone and firmness
  • Safe for most skin types
  • Can be combined with PRP (platelet-rich plasma)

Laser Treatments

We offer a variety of laser-based treatments for pigmentation, redness, thread veins, and skin resurfacing.

  • Clinically proven results
  • Precise targeting of problem areas
  • Minimal downtime with most systems

Medical-Grade Skincare Plans

We also develop personalised skincare routines using clinically active products to help you maintain and enhance your results at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a cosmetic dermatologist and a beautician?

A cosmetic dermatologist is a medically qualified skin specialist who can assess and treat the skin safely and effectively. They’re trained to recognise underlying medical issues, prescribe treatments, and use advanced techniques with precision—something beauticians are not medically licensed to do.

Are cosmetic dermatology treatments safe?

Yes—when performed by a qualified consultant dermatologist using medically approved products and equipment. At our clinic, all treatments are evidence-based and tailored to your individual skin type and needs, with safety as our top priority.

How long do cosmetic treatments last?

This depends on the treatment. Anti-wrinkle injections typically last 3–4 months, while dermal fillers can last 6–18 months. Skin treatments like peels or laser sessions often require a course for best results, followed by maintenance every few months.

Will my results look natural?

Absolutely. Our approach is subtle and refined—enhancing your natural features, not altering them. We aim to help you look like the best version of yourself, not someone else entirely.

What’s the best treatment for skin texture or acne scarring?

This depends on the depth and type of scarring or uneven texture. Microneedling, chemical peels, or laser resurfacing are all effective options. Your dermatologist will assess your skin and recommend the most appropriate treatment or combination.

Is there any downtime with cosmetic dermatology?

Many treatments have little to no downtime. Some procedures, like microneedling or laser, may cause mild redness or flaking for a few days. We’ll always let you know what to expect and provide aftercare guidance to support your recovery.

Can I combine different treatments?

Yes, many patients choose a combination approach—such as anti-wrinkle injections with skin resurfacing or fillers with microneedling—to address multiple concerns. This can enhance your overall result and extend the benefits of each treatment.

Are treatments suitable for all skin tones?

We treat patients of all skin tones and take care to choose the safest and most effective treatments for your individual skin type. Some laser treatments are not suitable for darker skin tones, but we offer many alternatives.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

That’s exactly what your consultation is for. Your dermatologist will examine your skin, listen to your goals, and guide you through your options—ensuring you feel informed and confident about your plan.

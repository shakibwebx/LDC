Clear + Brilliant Laser Clinic in London

The Clear + Brilliant laser treatment is a revolutionary non-invasive procedure that combats the natural effects of aging on the skin. This gentle treatment helps improve dull, sun-damaged skin by creating a more even skin canvas. The Clear + Brilliant laser treatment is most often used to help prevent the signs of ageing and to reduce the appearance of open pores. It can also reduce the appearance of scars, excess pigmentation, tighten pores, and improve overall skin texture and tone. The procedure is comfortable, affordable, and safe for all skin tones. How does the Clear + Brilliant laser work?

The laser used in the Clear + Brilliant treatment is non-ablative, meaning that it heats up the target tissue without actually destroying it. This makes the Clear + Brilliant treatment and other non-ablative lasers much less invasive than ablative laser treatments, such as the Sciton ProFractional laser. Clear + Brilliant works by creating an array of microscopic lesions across the surface of the skin using the heat from the laser. These microscopic lesions are surrounded by large areas of healthy skin, promoting quick healing with little to no downtime. The healthy skin helps speed up the healing of the tiny lesions within a matter of days, revealing healthier, smoother skin below. The damage to the skin stimulates the production of collagen, which helps to plump and smooth out imperfections in the skin, such as acne scarring.

What happens on the day of my Clear + Brilliant laser treatment?

Firstly, a local anaesthetic will be applied to the area about 30 minutes prior to treatment. Then, the Clear + Brilliant treatment will be carried out by one of our highly trained specialists. The laser treatment itself takes between 20 to 30 minutes. During the treatment, you may feel a slight warm sensation. After the treatment, there will be redness on the treated areas. This will begin to subside within a few hours, lasting up to 48 hours. The procedure is quick and an ideal alternative to several more intensive treatments, as its gentle laser means minimal downtime.

What areas of the body can be treated with the Clear + Brilliant laser?

The Clear + Brilliant laser is most frequently applied to the face for facial rejuvenation, effectively targeting fine lines, uneven skin tone, and texture. However, this versatile treatment can also be used to rejuvenate the skin in other areas such as the neck, chest, hands, and other parts of the body. The treatment provides a refreshing boost to the skin, enhancing its overall appearance by promoting a more even skin tone and smoother texture. Whether you aim to reduce signs of ageing, diminish scars, or improve pigmentation, the Clear + Brilliant laser offers a comprehensive solution for various skin concerns across multiple areas of the body.

How many Clear + Brilliant laser treatment sessions will I need?

We recommend a series of 4 to 6 laser treatments given 2 weeks apart. The number of treatments you need will be determined during your consultation. The specialist will help you develop a personalised treatment plan based on the results you want to achieve and the area or areas you want treated. If you are satisfied with the results after fewer sessions than recommended, you can decide to stop the treatments or continue with the sessions—it's entirely up to you. Many patients choose to make the Clear + Brilliant laser treatment a part of their regular skin care regimen to maintain a youthful appearance and radiant glow. The gentle nature of the laser allows patients to have the treatment as often as every 2 weeks at our dermatology clinic in London.

Is Clear + Brilliant laser treatment safe for my skin?

Yes, Clear + Brilliant is a safe treatment for your skin. It was approved by the FDA in May 2011 and has since been widely adopted by dermatologists nationwide. The non-ablative laser used in Clear + Brilliant targets the underlying skin layers without damaging the surface. This allows the top layer of skin to remain intact, resulting in a low-risk treatment with minimal downtime and quick healing. The safety and effectiveness of Clear + Brilliant make it a popular choice for those seeking to rejuvenate their skin while maintaining a high level of safety. Additionally, the gentle nature of the laser makes it suitable for all skin types, providing a versatile option for facial rejuvenation.

Who should not have treatment with Clear + Brilliant laser?

Although Clear + Brilliant is safe for the majority of people, there are certain conditions that make the treatment unsuitable. We recommend avoiding this laser treatment if any of the following conditions apply to you:

  • Pregnant
  • Currently taking oral retinoids
  • Having significant redness in the area of treatment from skin conditions such as actinic keratoses, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, etc.
  • Treating skin that is already recovering from a cosmetic procedure (e.g., chemical peel, laser resurfacing, etc.)
  • Have had a Botox or dermal filler procedure within the past two weeks

If you are planning to have Botox or dermal filler as well as the Clear + Brilliant treatment, we will start with the Clear + Brilliant and then continue with the Botox or the filler. If you have already had Botox or filler treatments, we recommend waiting two weeks before undergoing the Clear + Brilliant procedure.

How long does the treatment take?

The actual Clear + Brilliant treatment itself takes between 15 to 20 minutes. However, we recommend arriving 30 minutes prior to your appointment to allow time for the application and effect of the anaesthetic cream. This preparation helps ensure your comfort during the procedure. The application of the anaesthetic cream is included in the cost of the treatment, ensuring you have a smooth and pain-free experience. With the anaesthetic cream applied beforehand, the entire process, from arrival to completion, takes approximately 45 to 50 minutes. This minimal time commitment makes it an ideal treatment for those with busy schedules.

Does Clear + Brilliant laser hurt, and what will my face look like after the treatment?

During the treatment, you may feel a warm sensation in the treated area. A topical anaesthetic cream is applied beforehand to minimise any discomfort. Although your skin will look red after the treatment, this redness should resolve within 12 to 24 hours.

How long does it take to see results?

Results can be seen as early as the same day; your skin will feel smoother and appear more refreshed and radiant that evening. One of the many advantages of Clear + Brilliant is that after completing the recommended series of treatments, results do not fade if proper skin care is practiced. Patients are advised to continue wearing sunscreen to prevent further pigmentation and sun damage. We recommend coming in for touch-up treatments twice a year (or before special events) to maintain your results.

How should I prepare for the Clear + Brilliant laser treatment?

To prepare for your Clear + Brilliant treatment, avoid retinoids and exfoliating agents at least two weeks before your appointment to minimise skin sensitivity. Minimise sun exposure and avoid tanning beds for two weeks prior to the treatment. Stay well-hydrated and refrain from other cosmetic procedures on your face, such as waxing or microdermabrasion, for one week before your session. Use a gentle skincare routine and discuss any medications or treatments with your dermatologist.

How to care for your skin after your Clear + Brilliant laser treatment?

Clear + Brilliant is designed to minimise the side effects of traditional laser treatments. Immediately following your procedure, you may experience transient redness and swelling. We recommend not applying makeup for the rest of the day. The redness and swelling will resolve by the next morning. You may continue with your daily routine, but we do not recommend activities that involve sweating. Patients should avoid the gym, sauna, steam room, and facial or massage treatments on the day following your treatment. As the skin begins to heal, you may notice dryness. We recommend using a bland moisturiser to lessen any dryness experienced after your treatment.

Exposure to the sun before and after the treatment should be avoided. The skin is more sensitive to the sun following treatment, and sun exposure can increase the risk of unwanted pigmentation. After treatment, patients are urged to apply a high SPF sunscreen (30 or higher) daily.

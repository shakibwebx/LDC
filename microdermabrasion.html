Microdermabrasion in London

Microdermabrasion is a highly effective treatment for achieving a fresh, youthful glow. It is one of the most popular treatments offered at our dermatology clinic in London, capable of transforming your complexion and giving you newfound radiance. Microdermabrasion is a non-invasive procedure with no downtime, making it a convenient option for many skin concerns. It can address various issues such as fine lines, acne scars, uneven pigmentation, clogged and enlarged pores, and uneven skin texture.

Microdermabrasion is an advanced exfoliation technique that uses medical-grade polished crystals to refine the skin. This procedure accelerates the production of collagen and elastin, allowing your skin to appear naturally more youthful. For over 30 years, microdermabrasion has been effectively used to treat wrinkles and fine lines as part of comprehensive anti-ageing programmes. Our dermatology clinic utilises top-quality, medical-grade microdermabrasion machines, ensuring excellent results. All treatments are conducted by highly trained doctors who specialise in cosmetic procedures.

How Does Microdermabrasion Work?

Microdermabrasion is an excellent treatment for achieving fresher, younger-looking skin. It addresses a variety of skin concerns, including fine lines, blackheads, acne scars, stretch marks, age spots, large pores, pigmentation issues, and sun damage. This deep exfoliation technique employs medical-grade aluminum oxide crystals to gently remove the top layer of skin. This outer layer consists of dead skin cells, beneath which lies a layer of fresh, maturing skin. The procedure effectively buffs away this dead layer, which is often the source of minor skin imperfections such as fine wrinkles and acne scars. By breaking down the top layer of skin, microdermabrasion stimulates the production of new collagen and reveals the healthier, more radiant skin underneath.

During a microdermabrasion session, a specialised device directs a stream of fine crystals at the skin's surface. Simultaneously, the vacuum mechanism within the tool head removes the crystals and the exfoliated dead skin cells. The vacuum gently lifts a small section of skin towards the tool, bringing impurities closer to the surface for easier removal. With the dead skin layer removed, the skin becomes significantly more receptive to topical treatments. Many patients choose to enhance their microdermabrasion session by applying a Hydrating Vitamin Serum afterwards. This serum is absorbed more deeply into the skin, providing antioxidant protection, boosting collagen production, and delivering essential hydration for optimal skin health.

What Skin Conditions Can Microdermabrasion Treat?

Microdermabrasion is an effective treatment for a variety of skin conditions, significantly enhancing the overall texture and appearance of the skin. It can reduce the visibility of fine lines, blackheads, acne scars, stretch marks, age spots, enlarged pores, pigmentation issues, and sun damage. This procedure is also beneficial for improving skin texture and may offer relief to patients with rosacea. Many individuals opt for microdermabrasion to rejuvenate their skin, achieving a fresher, more youthful look. It is also a popular choice for those preparing for special events, ensuring their skin looks its best. By promoting the growth of new skin cells and stimulating collagen production, microdermabrasion provides a noticeable boost to skin health and appearance.

Is Microdermabrasion Painful?

Microdermabrasion is a gentle, non-invasive procedure that does not cause pain, and no anaesthetic is required. During the treatment, as the crystals exfoliate the dead skin cells, you may experience a mild warming sensation on your skin. This sensation is generally well-tolerated and many patients describe it as similar to the feeling of a massage. The procedure is designed to be comfortable and effective, allowing you to enjoy the benefits of smoother, healthier skin without any significant discomfort.

What Happens After the Microdermabrasion Procedure?

After the microdermabrasion treatment, you will have a fresh, new layer of skin. This new skin can be more sensitive to the sun, so it's important to apply sunblock regularly. Using moisturising creams will also help to soothe the skin and keep it hydrated. With the removal of the dead top layer of your skin, this is an ideal time to apply a Hydrating Vitamin Serum for optimal absorption. Your doctor may also recommend a prescription-only Retinoid Cream to enhance collagen production. Many patients find that they can have the procedure in the late afternoon and be ready to attend a dinner party or event in the evening, looking rejuvenated and refreshed.

Post-Microdermabrasion Facial Care

  • Avoid wearing makeup for at least six hours after your session.
  • Refrain from exposing the treated area to heat or hot water for twelve hours.
  • Apply sunscreen when outdoors for two weeks post-treatment. Sunscreen options are available in our clinic or online, and we suggest one that is non-comedogenic.
  • Use a facial moisturiser to maintain hydration as your skin may feel particularly dry after the procedure. We offer a specially formulated moisturiser that hydrates without clogging pores and supports the shedding of dead skin cells, which you can purchase on the day of your treatment.

Is Microdermabrasion Effective for Acne?

Microdermabrasion can be beneficial for reducing the appearance of acne scars. However, it is not advisable to undergo microdermabrasion if you have active acne. This is because the procedure can potentially spread acne-causing bacteria across the face, worsening the condition. For those experiencing active breakouts, we suggest consulting with one of our expert dermatologists who will recommend the appropriate treatment for your individual situation. To reduce the effects of acne scarring, microdermabrasion can be combined with advanced laser treatments such as Fraxel, which we offer. The best approach is to book a consultation with one of our specialists to discuss the most suitable treatment plan for you.

Microdermabrasion is ideal for anyone looking to refresh and rejuvenate their complexion without undergoing laser therapy. It’s an excellent way to enhance your skin's appearance through a very non-invasive treatment. This procedure is perfect for those who want to brighten, smooth, and even out their skin tone. Additionally, microdermabrasion can help unclog pores, providing a deep clean that removes dead skin cells and dirt, thereby preventing blemishes and breakouts. It's particularly effective for those who want to rejuvenate their complexion before a special occasion, offering a more intense treatment than a spa facial by using medical-grade crystals for deep exfoliation. However, if you have specific concerns such as acne scars or hyperpigmentation, you may benefit from a more targeted treatment.

Why Choose Our Dermatology Clinic?

Expert Care

Our renowned dermatologists are experts in treating a wide range of skin conditions, including acne, eczema, psoriasis, vitiligo, skin cancer, scarring, and pigmentation issues.

Extensive Experience

With over 19 years of service, we are one of London's most established dermatology clinics. Over the years, we've earned the trust of a loyal client base, including prominent figures and royals, both locally and internationally.

Aesthetic Services

We provide a variety of anti-ageing and skin rejuvenation treatments. Consultations are available to determine the suitability of treatments such as fillers, fraxel, liposonix, peels and laser hair removal.

Specialist Services

Our private dermatology clinic offers specialised services for conditions like melanomas, vitiligo, allergies, folliculitis, scarring, paediatric dermatology, and more.

Tailored Care

Our friendly staff will help match you with the dermatologist or clinician best suited to your needs.

On-Site Pharmacy

Our in-house pharmacy allows for immediate dispensing of medications following consultations, and we often perform procedures such as biopsies on the same day.

Advanced Diagnostics

We use cutting-edge technology for allergy patch testing, skin cancer screenings, and mole mapping for continuous monitoring.

Quick Test Results

Our partnerships with local laboratories ensure that biopsy and blood test results are typically available within 1-2 days.

Finance & Health Insurance

At The London Dermatology Centre, we are committed to ensuring that top-quality dermatological care is available to all.

We accommodate a wide range of insurance policies to make your path to improved health as smooth as possible. Our team is dedicated to helping you manage the financial details, so you can concentrate on what really matters — your health and comfort.

Our Location

Located at 69 Wimpole Street in the heart of Marylebone's Harley Medical District, central London, our dermatology clinic offers excellent accessibility.

The clinic is easily reachable with superb transport connections and a variety of local amenities, facilitating straightforward and stress-free visits to The London Dermatology Centre.

Bond Street Station is a brief seven-minute walk away, while Oxford Circus and Baker Street Stations are approximately a ten-minute walk. For visitors arriving by car, there is plentiful street parking nearby.

Opening Hours Notice

The London Dermatology Centre remains open for face-to-face appointments. The safety of our patients and staff is our number one priority. Safety measures are in place where we are limiting the number of patients and staff in the clinic at any one time. Hand sanitizers are available throughout the clinic, all patients MUST wear face mask/face coverage when attending appointments and PPE worn by staff. Please follow these guidelines as requested.

If you are feeling unwell, however mild it is – please do not enter the building. Stay home and protect our community.

You can book in for a video or phone consultation if your condition can be managed virtually, you can arrange this with our staff over the phone or via email. These consultations are charged at the same price as in-clinic appointments.

Our phone lines are open Monday-Thursday 9am-8pm, Fridays 9am-6.30pm and Saturdays 10am-1pm. You may also contact us at reception@the-dermatology-centre.co.uk

Thank you for all your support during this difficult time and we look forward to seeing you all again.

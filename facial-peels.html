Facial Peels London

At The London Dermatology Centre, we provide a variety of facial peels tailored to address numerous skin concerns. Facial peels are an excellent way to maintain a refreshed skin texture and tone. They are particularly effective for treating fine lines, acne scars, active acne, photoaging, pigmentation issues, and for overall skin brightening. The treatment works by exfoliating dead skin cells from the surface layer of the skin, promoting the growth of new, healthy cells. This results in a revitalised and radiant complexion. During your initial consultation at our dermatology clinic in London, a comprehensive assessment of your skin will be conducted. This allows you and your consultant to discuss any concerns and expectations related to the treatment. Our skilled aesthetic specialists will then walk you through the various peel options available, helping you choose the one that will achieve the best results for your specific skin type and needs.

Obagi Blue Peel: What is it?

The Obagi Blue Peel is an advanced treatment that uses Trichloroacetic Acid (TCA) to precisely control the depth of exfoliation. The peel's blue base acts as a guide, helping the practitioner monitor the application depth and adjust treatment areas as needed. This customisable peel is suitable for all skin types and can be effectively applied to the face, neck, chest, hands, and arms. The treatment typically takes up to 30 minutes, depending on the area being treated. Before the procedure, you will undergo a comprehensive pre-assessment with your consultant, providing an opportunity to address any questions or concerns. This ensures that the treatment is tailored to your specific needs, delivering optimal results.

Why Consider This Skin Peel?

The Obagi Blue Peel is an effective treatment for resurfacing facial skin affected by severe conditions such as:

  • Lines and wrinkles
  • Pigmentation variance over the face
  • Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation following acne
  • Deep wrinkles due to sun damage
  • Lentigo solaris
  • Actinic keratosis

At our dermatology clinic in London, you will receive a thorough consultation where a assessment is performed, and any issues related to your treatment are discussed with your consultant. This consultation helps determine the most suitable peel for achieving the best results. If the Obagi Blue Peel is selected, your skin will be prepared using specific pre-treatment preparations to create an optimal base for the peel.

The depth of a peel depends on the specific skin condition being addressed. The skin has two main layers: the epidermis on top and the dermis beneath. Skin thickness varies as thin, thick, or normal.

  • Light Exfoliation: This peel targets the top layer, the stratum corneum, helping to reduce fine lines, large pores, stubborn blackheads, and minor pigmentation issues. Healing time varies for each person.
  • Moderate Exfoliation: This peel reaches the basal layer of the epidermis, effectively treating deeper lines, acne scars, and pigmentation problems. The healing period is different for everyone.
  • Standard Peel: Penetrating the papillary dermis, this deep peel addresses marked pigmentation, deep acne scars, and severe wrinkles. Healing times vary by individual.
  • Advanced Peel: Reaching the reticular dermis, this very deep peel treats severe pigmentation issues and resistant acne scarring. The duration of healing depends on the individual's skin condition.

Glycolic Peel in London

A glycolic peel is a skin treatment designed to stimulate the growth of new skin and collagen by removing the superficial layer of dead skin cells. This outermost layer often contributes to a dull and lacklustre complexion. By exfoliating this top layer, the peel reveals fresher, more vibrant skin underneath. The glycolic peel is a mild yet effective treatment that, when used in conjunction with prescribed skin care products, can enhance the overall health and appearance of your skin. As part of a comprehensive treatment programme, a glycolic peel can rejuvenate your skin, giving it a healthy glow and a more youthful look. This treatment is suitable for various skin types and can address a range of skin concerns, including fine lines, acne scars, and uneven pigmentation. Get in touch with us now if you are interested in glycolic peel treatment in London.

Mesoeclat Skin Peel

Mesoeclat is a comprehensive skin peel system designed to rejuvenate and revitalise the skin. It comprises Salicylic Acid along with pre and post-peel supplements that are used at home as part of the treatment course. This system works to reveal new, healthier, and younger-looking skin by exfoliating the top layer of skin. Mesoeclat includes ingredients that are crucial for skin structure growth, such as elastin and collagen. The peel removes a layer of the skin, unveiling a smoother, more radiant complexion. The treatment results in a refreshed appearance with fewer wrinkles and firmer facial skin. As part of the Mesoeclat peel process, you will be prescribed a range of skincare products to prepare your skin before the treatment. These preparations create an optimal base for the peel to work effectively. The combined approach of in-clinic treatment and at-home care ensures comprehensive skin rejuvenation and long-lasting results.

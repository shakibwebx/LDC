Camilla Dalla Stella

Registered Dietitian

Camilla Dalla Stella is a recognized Registered Dietitian with a special interest in Clinical Nutrition. In 2017, she became a member of the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC) and the British Dietetic Association (BDA) in the United Kingdom, where she has been practicing since.

More Information

Her clinical work focuses on general clinical nutrition with a special interest in weight loss, weight loss surgery, diabetes, and gut diseases such as IBS, diverticulitis, Crohn's disease, autoimmune diseases, and inflammatory diseases like fibromyalgia and arthritis. She is skilled in managing both type 1 and type 2 diabetes and is an expert in metabolism and physiology.

Camilla completed her dietetics degree at Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Paraná (Catholic University in Paraná State, Brazil) in 2005. She later completed two years of clinical nutrition postgraduate training at Hospital Beneficência Portuguesa in São Paulo, Brazil, in 2007. Continuing her education, she pursued a master’s degree in Metabolism and Physiology at the Universitat de Barcelona in 2009. She undertook higher specialist training in obesity at NYU Langone and Presbyterian Hospital in New York, USA, and weight loss surgery at Maryland Hospital in Baltimore in 2012. She also gained skills in sports nutrition at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm in 2016.

In addition to her clinical expertise in private clinics, Camilla joined the Obesity and Weight Loss Surgery department at Chelsea and Westminster Hospital, NHS Trust, in 2023. Currently, she operates her own private practice.

Camilla also runs an online detox project, where she offers guided detox programs to help individuals rejuvenate their bodies and improve their overall health. Through this project, she creates and shares healthy recipes tailored to support detoxification, making it easy for her followers to adopt cleaner eating habits. Her recipes focus on using fresh, whole foods to provide essential nutrients while eliminating harmful substances. Camilla's online platform has become a valuable resource for those looking to enhance their wellness journey with nutritious and delicious meal options.

Camilla is dedicated to promoting healthy habits and a quality lifestyle. She emphasizes the importance of balanced nutrition, regular physical activity, and mental well-being as the foundations for a healthy life. Through personalized dietary plans and ongoing support, she helps her clients achieve sustainable health improvements and long-term wellness.

Opening Hours Notice

The London Dermatology Centre remains open for face-to-face appointments. The safety of our patients and staff is our number one priority. Safety measures are in place where we are limiting the number of patients and staff in the clinic at any one time. Hand sanitizers are available throughout the clinic, all patients MUST wear face mask/face coverage when attending appointments and PPE worn by staff. Please follow these guidelines as requested.

If you are feeling unwell, however mild it is – please do not enter the building. Stay home and protect our community.

You can book in for a video or phone consultation if your condition can be managed virtually, you can arrange this with our staff over the phone or via email. These consultations are charged at the same price as in-clinic appointments.

Our phone lines are open Monday-Thursday 9am-8pm, Fridays 9am-6.30pm and Saturdays 10am-1pm. You may also contact us at reception@the-dermatology-centre.co.uk

Thank you for all your support during this difficult time and we look forward to seeing you all again.

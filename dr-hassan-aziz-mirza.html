Dr Hassan Aziz Mirza

GMC Number: 7540298

Dr Mirza is fully registered with the General Medical Council and is a Member of the Royal College of Physicians.

His specialist interests include aesthetics, cosmetic dermatology, anti-ageing, and well-being.

More Information

Dr Mirza's undergraduate training was at the National University of Science and Technology, where he qualified in 2013. He completed his postgraduate medical training in internal medicine in the United States and the United Kingdom. Currently, Dr Mirza is a Dermatology Registrar at prestigious London hospitals and serves as an aesthetics trainer at the Harley Street Institute in London.

Integration of Modern Medicine and Aesthetics: Dr Mirza is passionate about merging modern medicine with aesthetics to provide you with comprehensive and holistic treatment options. By combining medical advancements with the artistic touch of aesthetics, he strives to enhance your natural beauty and well-being through cutting-edge techniques while addressing underlying skin conditions.

Eastern Medicine and Mysticism: Dr Mirza takes pride in incorporating the ancient wisdom of Eastern medicine and mysticism into his practice. Embracing time-honoured principles that harmonise mind, body, and spirit, he believes in promoting wellness from within. This unique blend of Eastern philosophy and modern dermatology sets Dr Mirza apart, allowing him to offer you a truly transformative experience.

Treatments offered by Dr Mirza include:

Anti-wrinkle Injections: This treatment temporarily relaxes facial muscles to smooth out lines and wrinkles, providing a rejuvenated appearance.

Hair Loss Treatment: This can include medications, topical solutions, and surgical options like hair transplants, all aimed at stimulating hair growth and preventing further hair loss.

Dermal Fillers: These are injectable treatments used to restore volume, enhance facial contours, and smooth out deep wrinkles, giving the skin a plumper and more youthful look.

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy: PRP therapy involves injecting concentrated platelets from the patient's own blood to promote healing and rejuvenation of the skin, reduce wrinkles, and improve hair growth.

Polynucleotides: These are injectable treatments that help repair and regenerate skin tissue by stimulating collagen production and improving skin elasticity and hydration.

Laser Treatments: This process can be used to address various skin concerns such as pigmentation, scars, and hair removal, resulting in clearer and smoother skin.

Mole/Skin Tag Removal: This involves safely eliminating unwanted skin growths using methods like excision, freezing, or laser, improving skin appearance and comfort.

Skin Boosters: These are injectable treatments that hydrate and revitalise the skin from within, enhancing skin texture, elasticity, and overall radiance.

Chemical Peels: This involves applying a chemical solution to the skin, which exfoliates the outer layers and promotes the growth of new, smoother skin underneath.

He is dedicated to providing personalised care and the latest in dermatological aesthetic and anti-ageing treatments to help you look and feel your best.

Opening Hours Notice

The London Dermatology Centre remains open for face-to-face appointments. The safety of our patients and staff is our number one priority. Safety measures are in place where we are limiting the number of patients and staff in the clinic at any one time. Hand sanitizers are available throughout the clinic, all patients MUST wear face mask/face coverage when attending appointments and PPE worn by staff. Please follow these guidelines as requested.

If you are feeling unwell, however mild it is – please do not enter the building. Stay home and protect our community.

You can book in for a video or phone consultation if your condition can be managed virtually, you can arrange this with our staff over the phone or via email. These consultations are charged at the same price as in-clinic appointments.

Our phone lines are open Monday-Thursday 9am-8pm, Fridays 9am-6.30pm and Saturdays 10am-1pm. You may also contact us at reception@the-dermatology-centre.co.uk

Thank you for all your support during this difficult time and we look forward to seeing you all again.
